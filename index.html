DATALYTICS

Datalytics is a leading provider of IT solutions, catering to businesses of all sizes. The company was established in Nigeria in 2010 and offers a wide range of products and services. We help our clients solve their business challenges through digital transformation. We bring a wide range of digital transformation solutions: Software Engineering, Big Data and Cloud Computing, On-premises Servers and Storages, Data Analytics and Machine Learning and Geospatial Intelligence.
